# Data Bank System for Provincial Government of Bataan

Provincial Government of Bataan contains multiple offices and/or departments that supports the entire operation of the province. These departments oversee the local government units (LGUs) and ensure they are efficient and effective in carrying out their mandates. The department provides technical and financial assistance to LGUs. The department's functions include monitoring and controlling illegal activities, issuing public informations, etc. This consist of different kinds of data ranging from a simple .docx file up to a complex spreadsheets. OPPDC (Office of the Provincial Planning and Development Coordinator) are always coordinating with other offices and/or departments to get their reports for data analysis. This creates so much gap and so much time just by communicating with other departments. Also other departments may do the same but for different purposes, it is very inefficient in terms of service delivery and lack of transparency and accountability and creates data fragmentation and silos. That's why proposing a Data Bank System will solve this problem by transforming data collection from a `pull model (frequently requesting data to other depertments)` to `push model (departments upload data to the system on schedule)` which improves the effiency for planning and oversight functions in every departments.
